Biography

American fashion designer, artist, and DJ Virgil Abloh (1980–2021) was a trailblazing figure in the worlds of streetwear and high fashion. Virgil established Off-White, his own label, in 2012. Because Off-White successfully combined high fashion with streetwear in a way that appealed to youth culture, the brand went viral. As the first Black artistic director of Louis Vuitton in 2018, Abloh created history while continuing to conflate fashion, music, and art. Following a quiet fight with a rare form of cancer, Abloh passed away in 2021. He left behind a significant legacy that continues to have an impact on fashion and culture all over the world.
